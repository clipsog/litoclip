Creator Agreement

Terms and conditions for creators on LitoClips

Last updated: January 2026

1. Introduction

This Creator Agreement ("Agreement") is between you ("Creator") and LitoClips ("Platform"). By joining as a Creator, you agree to the following terms.

Summary: You create content, we help you get paid. Be honest, create quality content, and follow campaign guidelines. Simple.

2. Creator Eligibility

To participate as a Creator, you must:

  • Be at least 18 years old (or have parental/guardian consent)
  • Own or have rights to the social media accounts you connect
  • Have the ability to receive payments (PayPal, crypto, etc.)
  • Agree to comply with all Platform rules and guidelines

3. Content Submissions

When submitting content to campaigns:

  • Originality: You must create original content or have proper rights/permissions
  • Guidelines: Follow each campaign's specific requirements and guidelines
  • Quality: Maintain reasonable quality standards as expected by the campaign
  • Accuracy: Only submit content posted on accounts you own and have verified

4. Earnings and Payments

How you earn money:

  • Earnings are calculated using the RPM (Revenue Per Mille) rate set by each campaign
  • View counts are verified through our tracking systems
  • Earnings become available for withdrawal after verification and approval
  • Minimum withdrawal thresholds may apply
  • You are responsible for all taxes on your earnings

5. Prohibited Activities

The following activities will result in account termination and forfeiture of earnings:

  • View Manipulation: Using bots, paid views, view exchanges, or any artificial inflation
  • False Information: Misrepresenting account ownership or metrics
  • Multi-Accounting: Creating multiple accounts to game the system
  • Content Theft: Submitting content you don't have rights to
  • Platform Abuse: Exploiting bugs or vulnerabilities

6. Account Verification

We verify accounts through:

  • Bio verification codes on connected social accounts
  • Cross-referencing submitted content with verified accounts
  • Monitoring for suspicious activity patterns

7. Content Rights

Regarding content ownership:

  • You retain ownership of all content you create
  • You grant campaigns limited license to track and report on your content
  • Brands may feature top-performing content with attribution (if specified in campaign)

8. Termination

Either party may terminate this agreement:

  • You may deactivate your account at any time
  • We may suspend or terminate accounts that violate these terms
  • Pending legitimate earnings will be paid upon termination
  • Earnings from fraudulent activity will be forfeited

9. Dispute Resolution

If you have concerns about earnings, content, or account status:

  • Contact us through our official channels first
  • We will review disputes within 7 business days
  • Our decision on fraud-related matters is final

10. Changes to Agreement

We may update this agreement. Significant changes will be communicated via email or platform notification. Continued use after changes constitutes acceptance.

11. Contact

Questions? Reach out through:

  • Discord Community
  • Contact Form
  • @LitoClips on X

By creating an account on LitoClips, you acknowledge that you have read, understood, and agree to be bound by this Creator Agreement.
