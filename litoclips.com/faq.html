How LitoClips works and who it's for

Who It's For

Upload your content. We turn it into clips and post for you. You can also earn from sponsors directly on our platform.

Artists

More streams, loyal fan base, ticket & merch sales.

Streamers

Build up audience, brand deals, narrative building (PR).

Podcasts

Grow your audience, sponsors, new listeners.

Apps & TV Shows

User acquisition, awareness, downloads, build buzz, reach new viewers, viral moments.

How It Works

Upload content. Pick platforms and how many accounts. Add the angle or narrative you want. We create the accounts and post for you.

1. Upload

Add a link to your content (YouTube, drive, etc).

2. Choose Platforms

TikTok, YouTube, Instagram, X. Pick where you want to post.

3. Set Your Angle

Write the narrative or angle you want for the clips.

What happens next

We create branded accounts and post clips for you. Track every post and view in your calendar. After week 1, pay to keep going or turn on auto-charge.

Sponsors

Creators can earn from sponsors directly on our platform.

For Creators

Opt in to receive sponsor offers. Get paid per view when your posts are sponsored. Allow watermarks and get a coupon off your campaign.

For Sponsors

Deposit a budget. Pick campaigns to sponsor. Pay per view. When your budget runs out, sponsorship stops.
