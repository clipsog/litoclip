FAQ

Got Questions?

Everything you need to know about the platform.

How do creators get paid?

Creators earn based on RPM (Revenue Per Mille). When your clips get views, you earn money from the campaign's budget. Withdraw anytime with no minimum threshold.

How do brands start a campaign?

Sign up, deposit your budget, set your RPM rate, and describe what kind of clips you want. Creators will start making content within hours.

What's RPM?

RPM = Revenue Per Mille (1,000 views). If a campaign has $5 RPM and your clip gets 100K views, you earn $500. Simple math.

Is there a fee to join as a creator?

No. It's completely free to join and start making clips. We take a small platform fee from campaigns, not from creator earnings.

What kind of content can I clip?

Gaming streams, podcasts, vlogs, educational content - whatever campaigns are available. You pick the ones that match your style.

How do I track my earnings?

Your dashboard shows real-time view counts, earnings per clip, and total balance. Full transparency on every dollar earned.
